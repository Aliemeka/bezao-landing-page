A Simple Bookmark Manager

A clean and simple interface to organize your favorite websites. Open a new browser tab and see your sites load instantly. Try for free.

Features

Our aim is to make it quick and easy for you to access your favourite websites. Your bookmarks sync between devices so you can access them on the go.

  • Simple Bookmarking
  • Intelligent Search
  • Easy Sharing

Bookmarks in one click

Organize your bookmarks however you like. Our simple drag-and-drop interface gives you complete control over how you manage your favourite sites.

Intelligent search

Our powerful search feature will help you find saved sites in no time. No need to trawl through all of your bookmarks.

Share your bookmarks

Easily share your bookmarks and collections with others. Share a shareable link that you can send at a click of a button.
